# VendorBridge - Procurement & Vendor Management ERP

VendorBridge is a comprehensive Procurement & Vendor Management ERP system designed to digitize and streamline procurement operations. The platform enables organizations to manage vendors, RFQs, quotations, approvals, purchase orders, invoices, and procurement analytics through a centralized system.

## Features

### Authentication & User Management
- User Registration & Login
- Role-Based Access Control
- Forgot Password Functionality
- Secure Session Management

### Vendor Management
- Vendor Registration
- Vendor Categorization
- GST & Contact Information Management
- Vendor Status Tracking

### RFQ Management
- Create and Manage RFQs
- Vendor Assignment
- Attachment Support
- Deadline Management

### Quotation Management
- Vendor Quotation Submission
- Editable Quotations
- Delivery Timeline Tracking
- Pricing Management

### Quotation Comparison
- Side-by-Side Comparison
- Lowest Price Highlighting
- Vendor Rating Analysis
- Filtering & Sorting

### Approval Workflow
- Approval/Rejection Process
- Approval Remarks
- Workflow Tracking
- Status Monitoring

### Purchase Orders & Invoices
- Auto-generated Purchase Orders
- Invoice Generation
- Tax Calculation
- PDF Download & Printing
- Email Integration

### Analytics & Reports
- Procurement Statistics
- Vendor Performance Analysis
- Spending Reports
- Monthly Trends Dashboard

## User Roles

### Admin
- Manage Users
- Manage Vendors
- View Analytics

### Procurement Officer
- Create RFQs
- Compare Quotations
- Generate Purchase Orders
- Generate Invoices

### Vendor
- Submit Quotations
- Track RFQ Status
- View Purchase Orders

### Manager / Approver
- Approve or Reject Requests
- Monitor Procurement Workflows

## Project Workflow

1. Procurement Officer creates RFQ
2. Vendors submit quotations
3. Quotations are compared
4. Approval workflow initiated
5. Purchase Order generated
6. Invoice generated
7. Invoice emailed/printed
8. Activities tracked through analytics
